International Conference and Publication

Synthesis and Characterization of Zn2+ doped Ba2TiO4 Nanoparticles

Sep 2023; ISBN: 978-93-83409-82-2

Synthesis and Characterization of Zn2+ doped Ba2TiO4 nanoparticles to examine their structural properties and study their electrical properties such as Dielectric constant, AC electrical conductivity, Dielectric loss and targeting applications in quantum-compatible materials. I synthesized by using a microwave-assisted solvothermal method. I determined particle sizes of 31.69 nm, 55.61 nm, and 13.93 nm for pure, 5 wt%, and 10 wt% doped samples, respectively. I studied their AC conductivity, dielectric constant, and dielectric loss across temperatures ranging from 40°C to 150°C and frequencies from 100 Hz to 1 MHz.

Synthesis and Characterization of Mn2+ doped CdS Nanoparticles

Apr 2022; ISBN: 978-93-81658-34-5

Synthesized and characterization of Mn2+ doped CdS nanoparticles, examining dopant effects on bandgap and conductivity by using UV-Vis Spectroscopy to assess their semiconductor behaviour. I used a solvothermal technique to create pure CdS nanoparticles and Mn2+ doped CdS nanoparticles (2.5 weight percent and 5 weight percent) as part of a separate research work. I characterized the grain size of the samples which are 21.73 nm, 22.80 nm, and 19.33 nm, respectively and analyzed their optical properties with UV-Vis spectroscopy, determining direct bandgaps of 2.04 eV, 2.00 eV, and 1.97 eV.
